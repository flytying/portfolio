## The Problem

Users lacked a simple, localized tool for managing personal finances in Philippine Peso. Existing apps were either too complex or not tailored to the local market.

## Research & Discovery

Surveyed 200+ potential users and ran competitive analysis on 8 finance apps. Created user personas and journey maps to understand spending habits and financial goals.

## Solution

Built a clean, intuitive mobile app with automated expense categorization, visual budget tracking, and gamified savings goals. Designed for simplicity with a focus on daily use.

## Results & Impact

- 4.7 star rating on initial beta testing
- 82% daily active user retention after 30 days
- Users saved an average of 15% more compared to their pre-app habits
