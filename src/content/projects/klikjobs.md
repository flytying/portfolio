## The Problem

Job seekers struggled with a cluttered interface that made it difficult to find relevant positions. The application process had too many steps, leading to high drop-off rates.

## Research & Discovery

Conducted user interviews with 15 job seekers and 5 employers. Mapped the existing user journey and identified key pain points through usability testing sessions.

Key findings:
- 73% of users abandoned applications midway
- Search filters were underutilized due to poor discoverability
- Mobile experience was significantly degraded

## Solution

Streamlined the job search with smart filters, a simplified application flow, and a mobile-first responsive design. Introduced a "Quick Apply" feature that reduced application time from 8 minutes to under 2 minutes.

## Results & Impact

- 45% increase in completed applications
- 60% improvement in mobile engagement
- 3.2x increase in returning users within the first month
