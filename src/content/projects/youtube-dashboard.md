## The Problem

Content creators needed a simpler way to track their YouTube watch hours and monetization progress without navigating YouTube Studio's complex interface.

## Research & Discovery

Talked to 12 YouTube creators about their analytics habits. Identified that watch hour tracking for monetization eligibility was the primary use case.

## Solution

Built a focused dashboard showing watch hours progress, daily trends, and projected monetization eligibility date. Clean data visualization with actionable insights.

## Results & Impact

- Clean, focused interface for watch hour tracking
- Real-time progress toward YouTube monetization goals
- Used by 100+ content creators in beta
