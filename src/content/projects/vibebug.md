## The Problem

Existing bug tracking tools are either overly complex or too simplistic. Development teams needed a tool that balanced power with usability.

## Research & Discovery

Interviewed 20 developers and product managers across 8 teams. Analyzed common workflows in Jira, Linear, and GitHub Issues to identify friction points.

## Solution

Designed a streamlined bug tracking interface with smart triage, keyboard-first navigation, and contextual information that reduces context switching.

## Results & Impact

- Currently in active development
- Beta testing with 3 development teams
- Early feedback indicates 50% faster bug triage time
