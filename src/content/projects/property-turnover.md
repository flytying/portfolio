## The Problem

Property managers relied on paper checklists and scattered spreadsheets to manage unit inspections during tenant turnover, leading to missed items and costly delays.

## Research & Discovery

Shadowed property managers during 5 actual turnover inspections. Documented their workflow, tools, and communication gaps with maintenance teams.

## Solution

Created a digital inspection platform with photo documentation, task checklists, real-time progress tracking, and automated report generation. Designed for both desktop office use and on-site mobile use.

## Results & Impact

- Reduced turnover processing time by 40%
- 95% inspection completion rate (up from 72%)
- Eliminated paper-based reporting entirely
